Privacy Policy for FIRE Planning Calculators

Last Updated: February 21, 2026

FIRE Planning Calculators ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains that we do not collect, store, or share any of your personal information.

No Data Collection

FIRE Planning Calculators is designed to be 100% offline and private. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data, usage statistics, or analytics.

  • No Account Required: You can use all features of the app without creating an account.
  • No Tracking: We do not use cookies, advertising IDs, or any tracking technologies.
  • No Analytics: We do not track how you use the app or which features you access.
  • No Third-Party SDKs: The app does not include any third-party code that collects data.

Data Storage

Local Storage

All data you enter into the calculators (such as age, income, savings, and assumptions) is stored locally on your device using Apple's standard UserDefaults system. This data remains on your device and is not accessible to us.

iCloud Sync

If you are signed in to iCloud on your device, FIRE Planning Calculators uses Apple's iCloud Key-Value Store to synchronize your inputs across your own devices. This data is encrypted by Apple and stored in your personal iCloud account. We do not have access to this data. You can manage this data through your Apple ID settings.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device (and optionally in your personal iCloud account), you have full control over it.

To delete all data:

  1. Delete the FIRE Planning Calculators app from your device.
  2. This will remove all locally stored data.
  3. iCloud data is managed by Apple and can be deleted via your device's iCloud storage settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Since we do not collect your contact information, we advise you to review this page periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the support link on the App Store.
